Submit a level

Click on the rectangles below and type in stuff. Relocate your cursor over the rectangles and most of the times a little tip thing will appear explaining in more detail what you are supposed to put in the rectangle.

Guidelines:

  • Please make the level appropriate for school.
  • The level needs to be possible to complete, but it also can't be too easy.
    • There needs to be a destination that isn't blocked off.
    • The level shouldn't automatically let the player win without doing anything.
    • I will no longer accept automatically self-completing levels.
  • The level can't be too similar to any existing levels, including the default level.
    • However, I can update an existing level if you are the original author or if you credit them in the maker name (e.g. "Billy123 and XxVaccinatedGamer69xX").
  • I will no longer accept levels using the rage powerup.
